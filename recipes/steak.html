Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Italian spaghetti with beef mince meat in a tomato raghu sauce.

Containing Onions & Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 500g Mince Meat
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Bolognese Flavour Mix
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Cook the mince until brown
  2. Chop the onion and mushroom and cook until soft
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and flavour mix, and stir
  4. Place lid on saucepan and cook on low heat for 20 more minutes
