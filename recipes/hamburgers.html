Hamburgers

Description

Homemade bacon cheeseburgers made with onion.

Ingredients

  • 500g Mince Meat
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Burger Bun
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Place the mince in a large bowl and add one egg
  2. Mix in the chopped onions
  3. Work into four or five large balls
  4. Place them in frying pan and cook on each side for 10 mins
